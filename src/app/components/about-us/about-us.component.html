About Us

Welcome to FoodToOrder, your number one source for all things related to food delivery. We're dedicated to providing you the best online food ordering experience, with a focus on dependability, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2024, FoodToOrder has come a long way from its beginnings in Bangalore. When we first started out, our passion for food delivering drove us to work hard, and gave us the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over World, and are thrilled to be a part of the booming wing of the food industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
